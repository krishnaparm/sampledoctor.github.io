Know Your Doctor


"Dedicated Infertility Specialist, Obstetrician, and Gynecologist with advanced training in Reproductive Endocrinology and a commitment to comprehensive women's healthcare"

Dr. Palak Jadav is a dedicated Infertility Specialist, Obstetrician, and Gynecologist with a strong foundation in women’s health and reproductive medicine.

She completed her MBBS from Tver State Medical University, Russia, followed by an internship at BJ Medical College, Ahmedabad. She further pursued a Diploma in Obstetrics and Gynecology from the College of Physicians and Surgeons, Mumbai, strengthening her expertise in maternal and reproductive health.

Dr. Jadav began her career as a Consultant at Subdistrict Hospital, Mansa, Gandhinagar, where she gained extensive clinical experience. With a keen interest in fertility care, she went on to complete a Fellowship in Reproductive Endocrinology at NOVA WINGS, equipping her with advanced skills in infertility management and assisted reproductive techniques.

Currently, she has been associated with Indira IVF Centre, Gandhinagar, for over a year, where she continues to provide comprehensive care to couples struggling with infertility. Known for her patient-centered approach, she combines medical expertise with compassion, guiding each patient with empathy and evidence-based treatments.

Our Services

We offer a wide range of solutions to meet your business needs.

IUI

IUI (Intrauterine Insemination) is a fertility treatment where specially prepared, concentrated motile sperm are placed directly into a woman's uterus via a catheter around the time of ovulation.

IVF

IVF (In Vitro Fertilisation) is the process of surgically removing a woman's eggs and fertilizing them with a man's sperm in controlled laboratory conditions.

ICSI

ICSI (Intracytoplasmic Sperm Injection) is a specialized fertility treatment and a subset of IVF where a single sperm is directly injected into the cytoplasm of a mature egg to achieve fertilization.

Self Cycle IVF

Self-cycle IVF is a type of IVF where the embryo is created using the individual's own eggs and their partner's sperm, ensuring the child will have a direct genetic link to both parents.

Donor Treatment

Donor treatment, or donor-assisted fertility treatment, involves using donated eggs, sperm, or embryos to help individuals or couples achieve pregnancy when they cannot conceive with their own gametes or have certain genetic conditions.

Hysteroscopy

Hysteroscopy is a medical procedure where a doctor uses a thin, lighted instrument to examine the inside of the uterus. This minimally invasive procedure is used to diagnose the cause of issues like abnormal bleeding, infertility, or miscarriages, and to treat certain uterine conditions.

Laparoscopy

Laparoscopy, also known as keyhole surgery, is a minimally invasive surgical procedure that allows surgeons to examine, diagnose, and treat conditions in the abdomen and pelvis without making large incisions.
